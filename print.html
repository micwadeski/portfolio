Print & Packaging

Contrary to popular belief, print is not dead. Don't get me wrong, screens are great, but with print and packaging, designers are presented with the rare challenge to engage more than just the viewers vision. Special effects like custom die lines, foil stamping and embossing can help differentiate your product in a dense market of competitors. A bright red fill could be utilized on food packaging to make the mood of the brand feel bold and appetizing. An organic coffee shop may want to use eco-friendly, bio-degradable packaging for their beans to further support the stance of their brand. The point being, when designing for print, there are endless opportunities to amplify your brand's message, convey quality and create an emotional connection between your brand and consumers.
